Capabilities

Everything you need for frictionless attendance

From kiosk deployment to webhook integrations, FRaaS covers the full lifecycle.

🎯

Sub-second Face Recognition

Facenet512 embeddings + cosine similarity deliver accurate identification in under one second, even in varied lighting conditions.

🛡️

Anti-spoofing & Liveness

Built-in spoof detection rejects printed photos and screen replays. Every recognition request is validated for liveness before logging.

🔄

Smart IN / OUT Toggling

A single face scan automatically toggles between clock-in and clock-out based on the employee's last recorded status for the day.

🏢

Multi-tenant Isolation

Every organisation gets its own API key and isolated data space. Employees from one organisation are never visible to another.

📊

Attendance Analytics

Real-time dashboards, daily summaries, and filterable logs give HR teams instant visibility into who's in and who's out.

🔗

Webhook & API Integration

Push every attendance event to your HRMS, Slack, or payroll system in real time via configurable webhooks with signed secrets.

🖥️

Kiosk Mode

Deploy a full-screen kiosk on any device with a webcam. Auto-capture every 4 seconds with a cooldown prevents duplicate punches.

📤

CSV Export & Data Control

Export all attendance logs as CSV for payroll, audits, or compliance. Full data deletion available on demand.

🔐

Embed & External Login

Embed the FRaaS kiosk into any internal portal via iframe with a single shareable link. No installation required for employees.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complex hardware. No proprietary software. Just a webcam, an API key, and your people.

1

Register your organisation

Sign up and choose your subdomain — e.g. acme.fraas.io. Availability is checked in real time. Your isolated workspace and API key are provisioned the moment you submit.

2

Enrol your employees

Send employees a personalised registration link. They snap a selfie and their face embedding is stored securely — no photos retained after encoding.

3

Deploy a kiosk or embed

Open the kiosk URL on any screen with a webcam. Or embed it in your existing intranet with a single iframe tag.

4

Track attendance in real time

View live logs on your dashboard, receive webhook events, and export CSVs for payroll — all from one place.
